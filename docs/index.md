## About This Book

This book provides a practical, beginner-friendly introduction to merchandising concepts in the grocery retail industry. Whether you're new to the domain or supporting retail projects, this guide will help you understand the business processes, workflows, and systems that power grocery merchandising.

### Who Should Read This Book?

This book is designed for **Quality Engineers and IT professionals** who:

- ✅ Are new to the grocery retail domain
- ✅ Support testing, DevOps, or product teams in retail projects
- ✅ Want to understand business processes and workflows
- ✅ Need clarity on roles and responsibilities within grocery merchandising
- ✅ Want to understand major retail systems and technologies
