# Merchandising Core Concepts for the Grocery Business

Welcome to **Merchandising Core Concepts for the Grocery Business** – a comprehensive guide designed specifically for **Quality Engineers and IT professionals** who want to understand the grocery retail domain.

## About This Book

This book provides a practical, beginner-friendly introduction to merchandising concepts in the grocery retail industry. Whether you're new to the domain or supporting retail projects, this guide will help you understand:

- The end-to-end grocery merchandising lifecycle
- Key business processes and workflows
- Different business roles and their responsibilities
- Major retail systems and technologies
- How to connect business concepts with testing and quality engineering activities

## Who Should Read This Book?

This book is especially helpful for professionals who:

- ✅ Are new to the grocery retail domain
- ✅ Support testing, DevOps, or product teams in retail projects
- ✅ Want to understand business processes and workflows
- ✅ Need clarity on roles and responsibilities within grocery merchandising
- ✅ Want a high-level understanding of major products and software systems used in the industry

## Book Structure

Each chapter follows a consistent format for easy learning:

1. **Concept Explanation** – Clear definition in simple language
2. **Business Use Case** – Real-world example from a grocery business
3. **Users Involved** – Roles and responsibilities
4. **Tools & Technologies Used** – Major systems, software, and platforms
