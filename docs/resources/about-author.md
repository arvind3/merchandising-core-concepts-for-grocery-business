Arvind is a seasoned technology professional with over two decades of experience in IT, specializing in DevOps, automation, and product management. His career spans multiple industries including retail, where he has developed deep expertise in merchandising systems and quality engineering.

### Why This Book?

This book was born from Arvind's experience working with Quality Engineers and IT professionals who needed to quickly understand the grocery retail domain. Recognizing the gap between technical expertise and business domain knowledge, he created this resource to help technical professionals:

- Speak the same language as business stakeholders
- Understand the "why" behind merchandising workflows
- Design better tests and systems with domain context
- Accelerate their onboarding to retail projects
