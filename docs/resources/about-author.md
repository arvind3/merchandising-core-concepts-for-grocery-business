# About the Author

**Arvind Bhardwaj** is a senior technology and product leader with extensive experience delivering enterprise software and operational platforms.

This book was developed as a practical bridge between retail business operations and modern engineering execution. It is written for professionals who need to understand how merchandising decisions are made, measured, and sustained in large grocery environments.

## Why This Book

Grocery merchandising is often documented either as high-level business theory or system-specific implementation notes. The goal of this book is to connect strategy, operations, analytics, and engineering into one clear operating narrative.

## Intended Readers

- Merchandising and category professionals
- Product and program managers in retail technology
- Data and analytics teams supporting commercial decisions
- Quality engineers responsible for high-risk pricing and promotion workflows
